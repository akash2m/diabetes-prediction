About the Parameters

  • Age: The age of the patient. Risk increases with age.
  • Pregnancies: Number of pregnancies. Related to insulin sensitivity.
  • Glucose: Plasma glucose concentration after test. High value = risk.
  • Blood Pressure: Diastolic pressure. High pressure = higher diabetes risk.
  • Insulin: Abnormal insulin = possible resistance.
  • BMI: Body Mass Index. Obesity is a major risk factor.
  • Skin Thickness: Indicates body fat distribution.
  • DPF: Diabetes Pedigree Function. Indicates genetic influence.
